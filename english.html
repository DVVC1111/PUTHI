What is Puthi?

(PUTHI) Puthi is a campaign that aims to connect the younger generation to protect themselves on the Internet by sharing knowledge on the topic of cyber security in Cambodia.

Register

Our Vision

...

As technology advances, online security is becoming more and more important in everyday life. Online security is not just about protecting our electronic devices or online data, it is about protecting and managing our identity. Knowledge on the topic of cyber security in Cambodia is limited, which is why we initiated the idea of creating this campaign.

We aim to connect the younger generation to protect themselves online by sharing tips to protect their online identities. Puthi will provide free internet security training to protect yourself and your loved ones.
